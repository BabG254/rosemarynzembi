About Rosemary

Bridging Cultures, Inspiring Lives

From Matetani village to Dallas and back to Machakos in 2013, my journey has been one of continuous transformation. Starting with Faith & a huge dream from the village in the early 90s, my transition was a full circle. As East Africa's leading voice for authentic empowerment, I blend American innovation with African wisdom to unlock human potential.

Through Kandid Xpressions, I've helped thousands discover their authentic voice, overcome limiting beliefs, and step into their power. My mission is simple: to create a world where every story matters and every voice is heard.

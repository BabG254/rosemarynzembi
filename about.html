My Story

From Dallas to Machakos

A journey of transformation, authenticity, and purpose that led me to become East Africa's leading voice for empowerment.

Bridging Two Worlds

My journey began in Matetani village in the early 90s, armed with Faith & a huge dream! I always felt a deep connection to stories—both the ones we tell ourselves and the ones we share with the world. Little did I know that this passion would one day lead me across continents to discover my true calling.

My dream to serve others evolved in Dallas, Texas, which led me back to the village in 2013. This transition was a full circle - a journey that wasn't just geographical but a spiritual awakening. In Machakos, I found not only a return home but a deeper understanding of what it means to live authentically and serve others through the power of story.

Today, I blend the innovation and determination I learned in America with the wisdom, community spirit, and authentic expression that flows through African culture.

My Purpose

Mission

"To engage, inspire, motivate, educate, and liberate through authentic storytelling and transformative coaching."

Every interaction I have is guided by this mission. Whether I'm coaching a single mother in Nairobi, speaking to corporate leaders in Dubai, or sharing stories through my podcast, my goal remains the same: to help people discover their authentic voice and step into their power.

Vision

"A world where every individual recognizes and embraces their unique potential."

I envision communities across Africa and beyond where stories are shared without fear, where authentic connection leads to collective growth, and where every voice contributes to a symphony of positive transformation.

My Journey

Key milestones that shaped my path to becoming a voice for authentic empowerment.

Early Career in Dallas

2005-2010

Began my career in public relations and project management, discovering my passion for communication and human connection while working with diverse communities.

The Great Move

2011

Relocated to Machakos, Kenya, embracing new cultural experiences that would profoundly shape my perspective on authentic living and community connection.

Birth of Kandid Xpressions

2013

Founded my platform for authentic storytelling and empowerment, combining my passions for communication, coaching, and community building into one mission.

Professional Certification

2016

Completed professional coaching certification, adding structured methodologies and proven frameworks to my natural abilities and intuitive approach.

Leading Voice of Empowerment

Present

Now recognized as East Africa's leading voice for authentic empowerment, combining coaching, speaking, writing, and podcasting to transform lives across continents.

My Core Values

Empowerment

Connection

Stories bridge hearts and minds. Through genuine human connection, we create communities that support and celebrate each other's growth.

Continuous Growth

Life is a journey of constant evolution. I'm committed to my own growth while supporting others in their transformative journey.

Purpose-Driven

Every action should be rooted in purpose. I help others discover their unique calling and align their lives with what truly matters.

Cultural Bridge

I celebrate the beauty of cultural diversity, blending American innovation with African wisdom to create inclusive paths to empowerment.

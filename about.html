My Story

From Dallas to Machakos

With more than twenty-five years of combined professional experience, Rosemary is a philanthropist at heart who has become a force to reckon with among her circles of influence, both in the United States and East Africa. Her compassion for humanity fuels much of her speaking career, which she often does from a non-profit perspective.

Bridging Two Worlds

My journey began in a remote village in Kenya, where I was abandoned at birth and raised by my widowed grandmother under harsh conditions. Growing up poor and fatherless, I knew all too well the pain of rejection and disillusionment, but I refused to become another village statistic.

At age 15, during a youth camp, my life took a different turn when I discovered my identity in Christ. My painful childhood experiences bore in me a deep compassion for the youth in our society, who often feel voiceless, misplaced and misunderstood. Through hard work, faith in God and belief in my own dreams, I determined to look beyond the shadow and forge my way into a life of purpose.

My warm electrifying personality makes it easy for audiences to connect with me, therefore taking in the best of my powerful teachings. I hold an Associates Degree in Applied Sciences (AASc) in Computer Information Technology and Bachelor of Applied Sciences (BASc) in Operations Management. I am also a Certified Professional Life Coach, a path which I tread with great passion, having mastered the art of resilience in the face of adversity.

My college education didn't come on a silver platter, but was hard earned through self-driven determination to soar above cultural stereotypes and triumph over mediocrity. Much of who I am today has been inspired by the success stories of other humanitarian enthusiasts who were social rejects, like Oprah Winfrey, Steve Jobs, the Late Nobel Prize Winner Prof. Wangari Maathai and Tyler Perry.

My Purpose

Mission

"To engage, inspire, motivate, educate, and liberate through authentic storytelling and transformative coaching."

Every interaction I have is guided by this mission. Whether I'm coaching a single mother in Nairobi, speaking to corporate leaders in Dubai, or sharing stories through my podcast, my goal remains the same: to help people discover their authentic voice and step into their power.

Vision

"A world where every individual recognizes and embraces their unique potential."

I envision communities across Africa and beyond where stories are shared without fear, where authentic connection leads to collective growth, and where every voice contributes to a symphony of positive transformation.

Professional Journey

Key milestones that shaped my path to becoming a voice for authentic empowerment.

Early Foundation & Education

Early Years - 2000s

Born into adversity but refused to be defined by circumstances. Overcame cultural stereotypes through self-driven determination, earning an Associates Degree in Computer Information Technology and Bachelor's in Operations Management through hard work and unwavering faith.

Twinkle Little Stars Foundation

2006

Founded Twinkle Little Stars, Inc., a US-based nonprofit organization (now Made To Thrive) committed to sharing the gospel of Truth, Hope and Love through sustainable youth development programs, driven by passion for poor and fatherless children.

Professional Life Coaching Certification

2009

Became a Certified Professional Life Coach (CPC), mastering the art of resilience in the face of adversity. This certification combined with lived experience created a powerful platform for helping others overcome self-imposed limitations and victim mentality.

Global Partnerships & Speaking Career

2005-2013

Built partnerships with organizations across the globe including Rotary International (USA), Big Brothers Big Sisters (USA), Healing Hands International (USA & Africa), and Kenya Literature Bureau, facilitating youth literacy and social/spiritual empowerment.

Return to Kenya - Full Circle Journey

2013

After studying, working and living in America for 12+ years, relocated to Kenya to impact young lives personally and from up-close. This return marked the beginning of hands-on, grassroots empowerment work combining American innovation with African wisdom.

Kandid Xpressions! Launch

2023

Launched Kandid Xpressions! as the comprehensive platform for authentic storytelling, professional coaching, and empowerment. This became the vehicle for sharing interactive, fun, practical and motivational training programs that help people reconnect with their true potential.

Leading Voice of Empowerment

Present

Now recognized as Sub-Saharan Africa's leading voice for authentic empowerment. Through custom-tailored presentations for conferences, schools, and businesses, helping audiences step out of their own way and soar to great heights like engineered hot air balloons.

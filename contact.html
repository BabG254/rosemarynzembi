Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with me.

How quickly can we start working together?

For coaching clients, I typically have availability within 1-2 weeks after our discovery call. Speaking engagements require 2-4 weeks advance notice, though I can accommodate urgent requests when possible.

Do you offer virtual coaching sessions?

Yes! The majority of my coaching clients work with me virtually via Zoom. This allows me to serve clients across Africa and internationally. In-person sessions are available for clients in the Nairobi area.

What languages do you speak?

I conduct sessions in English and Swahili. I can also incorporate basic Kikuyu for clients who would benefit from connecting with their cultural roots in their native language.

Do you travel internationally for speaking engagements?

Absolutely! I regularly speak at conferences and events across East Africa and am available for international engagements. Travel and accommodation costs are included in speaking fees for events outside Kenya.

What makes your coaching approach unique?

My approach combines modern coaching methodologies with African wisdom traditions. I help clients honor their cultural heritage while pursuing contemporary goals, creating authentic success that feels true to who they are.
